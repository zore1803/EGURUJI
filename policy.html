    a. Personal Information Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details. Payment information for appointment bookings. Location details (if provided) for service facilitation.

    To facilitate appointments between customers and pandits. To process payments and send appointment confirmations. To improve our website and services based on user behavior. To send notifications, updates, and promotional materials (if opted in). To ensure user security and prevent fraudulent activities.

With Customers: Basic details of the pandit (name, experience, contact) may be shared with the customer upon booking. With Third Parties: Payment gateways to process transactions. Service providers assisting with website operations. Legal authorities if required by law.

    We use encryption and secure servers to protect user data. Users must keep their login credentials confidential. While we take security measures, no online service is 100% secure.

    We use cookies to improve user experience and website functionality. Users can manage or disable cookies through their browser settings.
