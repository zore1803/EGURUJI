Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellation by Customer
    Before 48 hours of the scheduled service:
    If you cancel your booking more than 48 hours before the scheduled time, you will receive a full refund, minus any applicable payment gateway charges. Between 24 to 48 hours before the scheduled service:
    If you cancel your booking between 24 and 48 hours before the scheduled time, you will receive a 50% refund of the total booking amount. Within 24 hours of the scheduled service:
    Cancellations made within 24 hours of the scheduled time will not be eligible for a refund.
  • Rescheduling
    More than 48 hours before the scheduled service:
    You can reschedule your booking without any additional charge if the request is made more than 48 hours prior to the scheduled service. Within 24 to 48 hours of the scheduled service:
    Rescheduling within this period may incur a rescheduling fee of [X amount].
    Less than 24 hours before the scheduled service:
    Rescheduling requests within 24 hours of the scheduled time will be treated as a cancellation, and the above cancellation policy will apply.
  • Cancellation by E-Guruji
    In rare cases, we may have to cancel your booking due to unforeseen circumstances such as the unavailability of the pandit or extreme weather conditions.
    In such cases, you will be notified as soon as possible, and we will offer either a full refund or the option to reschedule at no extra charge.
  • No-Show Policy
    If the customer is unavailable or does not attend the puja at the scheduled time and location, it will be treated as a no-show and no refund will be provided.
  • Refund Processing
    Refunds, if applicable, will be processed within 7-10 business days. Please note that the amount may take additional time to reflect in your bank account, depending on your payment provider.
  • Special Cases
    For certain special services or events, a different cancellation policy may apply. These will be clearly mentioned during the booking process.
