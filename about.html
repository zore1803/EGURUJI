About Us

E-Guruji is an Online Puja Booking Portal where technology meets tradition. All our Pandits are trained in certified Vedic Pathashalas and they are experienced in performing puja professionally.

E-Guruji Offers:

  • Convenience to the client and process their requirement at the click of a button.
  • Use superior technology and build a robust and interactive online platform for clients.
  • Services of qualified Panditjis, whose antecedents (qualifications as well as behavioral) were checked and confirmed by experts.
  • A comprehensive and complete Puja samagri kit, in superbly branded Boxes and Bags.
  • Ensure complete transparency in pricing by displaying the prices directly on the website.
  • Pujas and Panditjis are serious business. But E-Guruji will bring about a celebratory emotion, quickness of service and yet maintain the austerity of the Puja.
  • Each of the Panditjis undergo a soft skills training program which E-Guruji arranges. They will be communicative. The Panditjis will be trained in etiquette and will conduct themselves in a manner, which has earlier never been experienced. The Pujas will be conducted in an inclusive and festive manner.
  • Our Pandit will be communicative.
  • Each of the Panditjis who visit client's homes for Pujas will be vetted for security reasons.
  • E-Guruji will encourage clients to post online reviews to ensure constant improvement.
